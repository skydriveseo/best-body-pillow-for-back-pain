A mattress serves two functions: support and comfort. A supportive mattress can make or break your spinal alignment. A large mattress is a popular choice for people who enjoy it. However, the mattress should provide support.

Your spinal health may be affected by how you sleep. Side sleepers are more likely to maintain a neutral spine position. However, if you lie on your back, choosing the best mattress brand that provides enough support is even more important.

If you lie on your back on a mattress that doesn't provide enough support, your spine will curve abnormally if you do. This can cause back pain. Supportive mattresses, on the contrary, won't allow your spine to sink in and will maintain its natural curve.
